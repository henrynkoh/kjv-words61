# KJV Words

KJV Bible verses by topic for discussing and spreading words of truth—by **audience**, **situation**, and **level of Bible knowledge**. Styled after [kjv-faith-ai](https://github.com/henrynkoh/kjv-faith-ai).

> "Study to shew thyself approved unto God, a workman that needeth not to be ashamed, rightly dividing the word of truth." — 2 Timothy 2:15 KJV

## Topics (24 categories)

- **Bible & inspiration** — How we know the Bible is the inspired Word; prophecy, Daniel, Revelation
- **Attributes of God** — Sovereign, grace, love, infinite, omnipresent, omniscient
- **Why God created us** — Eternal purpose, objects of His love, manifold wisdom
- **Unbeliever & God** — Carnal mind, enmity, cannot please God without faith
- **What is faith** — Taking God at His Word; just shall live by faith; propitiation
- **Who is the Lord Jesus Christ** — Word was God; image of the invisible God; I AM
- **Adam & Eve** — Why they ate; one man sin entered; death passed upon all
- **Why Christ died for us** — Free gift, justification, old man crucified
- **Only way to be saved (grace)** — Believe the gospel: died, buried, rose again
- **Saved in Old Testament times** — Abraham by faith; Abel, Noah; faith + sacrifice
- **Attributes of Satan** — Angel of light, god of this world, liar, principalities
- **Three temptations** — Lust of flesh, lust of eyes, pride of life
- **Two kinds of death** — Physical; spiritual (second death)
- **Where we go when we die** — Believers with Lord; unbelievers hell → lake of fire
- **Unsaved judged by works** — Great White Throne; books opened
- **Saved judged by works** — Bema seat; rewards; gold, silver, precious stones
- **When God lost the human race** — Adam; redemption, slave market
- **One reason God sends to Hell** — Unbelief; gospel hid; blinded minds
- **God of love & Lake of Fire** — He did everything; they refused to believe
- **Atheists & non-believers** — Believe in God vs believe God; fool says no
- **Eternal heaven and earth** — New Jerusalem, no more death or pain, nations
- **How to pray** — Thanksgiving, requests, peace; in Jesus’ name
- **How to know you’re saved** — Led by Spirit; love Word and saints; gospel
- **Key to understanding the Bible** — Rightly divide; to whom written; Israel vs Church

## Run

```bash
npm install
npm run dev
```

Open [http://localhost:3000](http://localhost:3000). Browse 24 topics and open any category to read KJV verses with theme and Korean (or English) summaries.
